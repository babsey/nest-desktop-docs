NEST Simulator
==============

How can I change the URL of the NEST Simulator?
   On the settings page you can find (and change) the URL of the NEST Simulator.

How can I check NEST Simulator?
   On the settings page you can click on a :bdg-dark-line:`CHECK` button.
   If a chip with NEST version appears, this indicates that the selected NEST Simulator is working.
